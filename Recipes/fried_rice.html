Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Butter
  • Veggies
  • Sesame Oil
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Soy Sauce
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Put refrigerated rice on pan
  2. Apply butter on pan
  3. Add vegetables while its cooking on high heat
  4. Add sesame oil and oyster sauce
  5. Let Rice brown a bit
  6. Take out and drizzle soy sauce
