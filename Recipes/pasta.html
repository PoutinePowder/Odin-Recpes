Pasta

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Meatballs

Steps

  1. Make your pasta like a real professional
  2. Boil the pasta in a pot, pretty obvious stuff
  3. Heat up the tomato sauce and meatballs all at once
  4. Once heated mix the pasta sauce with the finished pasta
  5. Apply some shredded cheese on top voila.
